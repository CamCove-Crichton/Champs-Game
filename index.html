How To Play:

  • Try to match the target number by guessing a number from 1 to 10 for level 1, from 1 to 20 for level 2 and from 1 to 30 for level 3, depending on the target number
  • Submit your answer once you are happy with your guess
  • The computer will then guess a number from 1 to 5 for level 1, from 1 to 10 for level 2 and from 1 to 15 for level 3, depending on the target number
  • If the sum of your number and the computers number equal the target number you get a point
  • If the sum does not match the target number, then the computer gets a point
  • The first to get to 10 points wins
